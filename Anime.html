What Is Cowboy Bebop

The story, which takes place in a post-apocalyptic future in which Earth has largely become uninhabitable in 2071, centers on a gang of licensed bounty hunters known as cowboys who pursue criminals, capture them alive, and then turn them in for a payment.






Characters

Characters Description Photo
Spike Spiegel Main Character: a bounty hunter on board the Bebop and an ex-Syndicate member. He is also an expert in flying, gunfights, and martial arts.He's also haunted by his past, as he yearns to reunite with a lovely woman from his past. He has a funny and aloof aspect to his personality.
Jet Black Side Character:Is a bounty hunter, but he's also known as the "Black Dog" because once he sinks his fangs into something, he won't let go. He's also the captain of the Bebop and an ex-ISSP Special Force Officer who enjoys American jazz and detective work.
Faye Valentine Side Character: A coma survivor attempting to restore her memory. With a lot of debt, she resorted to a life of crime and hustling to survive until she chose to live on the Bebop and become a bounty hunter.
Edward Side Character: Is a hacker prodigy and mechanical genius. Despite her eccentric personality and use of third person to speak.
